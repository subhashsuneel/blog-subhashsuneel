Diploma 4th semester – C language subject.

It was in 2017 when I was in my diploma 4th semester, I had a subject named Programming in C and MATLAB. (As per C-16 regulation, Andhra Pradesh, India)

And there was when I first started to get introduced to programming. There is not so much learning and practical stuff. They taught us some programming concepts in the C language. The syllabus at that time consists of 5 chapters.

  1. C Programming Basics
  2. Conditional statements and Arrays.
  3. Strings, Functions & pointers.
  4. Stuctures & unions.
  5. Basics of MATLAB.

And the story was like any other college subject we used to study and write our tests and get passed

And coming to the interesting part we are taken to laboratories once every week to practice C language on old blue colour nostalgic IDE’s and me and many used to see our lab manuals and just copy-paste the code, compile and run the program and show to our instructor and rest of the time we used to have fun with friends. And all I have done. Just came to know some basics of some programming terms.
